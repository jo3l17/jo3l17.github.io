The best places in Cozumel

Palancar Reef

If you want to dive, Cozumel is the best option, One of the best spots to dive is the Palancar Reef, rising from depths of up to 80 meters to shallows just beneath the surface of the sea at the southwest end of the island. Other good diving locales are on the San Francisco, Paraíso, and Maracaibo reefs, as well as the Santa Rosa Wall. And pretty much wherever you dive, you're certain to see such exotic species as angelfish, nurse sharks, and sea turtles.

San Miguel de Cozumel

San Miguel is the largest and main city of Cozumel, being head of the municipality of the same name and the second oldest city in the Mexican state of Quintana Roo.
It is one of the main tourist destinations in Mexico, mainly for cruises and its waters are famous for diving, it has the Cozumel International Airport, with national and international destinations and a ferry that serves for communication with Playa del Carmen and the rest of the continental surface.

Cozumel's Beaches

Enjoy the best views in Cozumel's beaches, you will enjoy in all the variety of beaches Cozumel can offer to you. Among the most popular and attractive of Cozumel's beaches are Playa San Juan and Playa Santa Pilar in the northwest corner of the island, and Playa San Francisco, Playa Santa Rosa, and Playa Palancar to the southwest.

Museum of the Island of Cozumel

It belongs to the Foundation of Parks and Museums of Cozumel, Quintana Roo, decentralized organism of the state government, since 2005. It specializes in the conservation and environmental education and history of the Island. It has four rooms: La Isla, El Mar , History and Archeology and Contemporary Cozumel 2 temporary exhibition halls and donors, a Maya Room, Casa Maya and a public library. Cultural and recreational artistic activities are presented in his auditorium Lic. Pedro Joaquín Coldwell.

Punta Sur

the perfect combination of beautiful beaches and contact with nature. In Punta Sur there are viewpoints located at strategic points from where, if you are lucky, you will be able to see these wonderful pink birds in their natural habitat. There are also pelicans, gulls, herons, eaglets and many more birds.
On the Punta Sur beach in Cozumel there is a wooden pergola with hammocks and lounge chairs for those who want to rest for a while in the shade
